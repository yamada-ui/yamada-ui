## 使い方

```tsx preview

    <NativeTable.Tr>
      <NativeTable.Th>作品名</NativeTable.Th>

      <NativeTable.Th numeric>話数</NativeTable.Th>
    </NativeTable.Tr>

  <NativeTable.Tbody>
    <NativeTable.Tr>
      <NativeTable.Td>ドラゴンボール</NativeTable.Td>

      <NativeTable.Td numeric>全153話</NativeTable.Td>
    </NativeTable.Tr>

  </NativeTable.Tfoot>

```
